An ode to the Moon

Sara Williams

more_horiz share favorite

24

In life, I know 2 things to be true: Crunchy peanut butter is better than creamy. I will be bored of what I am doing now in 6 months. I meet many people who say “I don’t like to work,” or (worse) “I’m not good at anything.” Many of the Internet gurus seem to assume there is one consuming passion out there, that you need only reach out your hand and find what you were meant to do for the rest of your life. At that point, all feelings of insufficiency will fade. The butterflies descend from above as you embrace your one true purpose for the next 30 years of your life. Consider this: you may not have an ideal job. So where do you begin your search?
